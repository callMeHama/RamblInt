WHO ARE WE?

We are a group of passionate ramblers. No more, no less. It all started in 2003 when a group of students from Missouri gave a thought and tried to make something to track countryside areas available for walks. In 2007, the same team started their first programme, bringing friends and family members to ramble in place in Louiziana. After many other ramblings, they founded the club known today as RambleInt.
